Intro: This globe shows the average temperature anomalies over land between the years of 1910 and 2019 in decades. Temperature anomalies are defined by the difference in temperature(Δ℃) at that location between at a given period in time and the average temperature(℃) between the years of 1951 and 1980 (chosen by NASA).

Directions: Travel to a specific period on Earth by clicking on a decade from the list in the middle left; observe how the differences in the temperature spikes.

Controls: Drag the mouse to rotate the scene | Drag the slider to scale
